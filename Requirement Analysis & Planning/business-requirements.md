# Understanding Business Requirements

## Salesforce AI-Powered Lead Generation System

## 1. Overview

The Salesforce AI-Powered Lead Generation System is designed to improve the efficiency of lead generation and management by integrating Salesforce CRM with Agentforce AI capabilities. The system analyzes customer information, website interactions, and business data to identify high-potential leads and assist sales representatives in prioritizing their efforts.

This project aims to reduce manual work, improve conversion rates, and enable organizations to make data-driven sales decisions.

---

## 2. Business Goals

- Improve the efficiency of lead generation.
- Increase the conversion rate of potential customers.
- Reduce manual effort in identifying and managing leads.
- Provide AI-driven recommendations for sales representatives.
- Enable data-driven decision-making.
- Improve customer engagement and response time.

---

## 3. Business Challenges

Organizations often face the following challenges:

- Manual identification of quality leads.
- Difficulty in prioritizing potential customers.
- Delayed follow-up with prospects.
- Lack of centralized customer information.
- Inefficient lead qualification process.
- Low sales productivity.

---

## 4. Proposed Solution

The proposed Salesforce AI-Powered Lead Generation System provides:

- Automated lead capture.
- AI-powered lead scoring.
- Intelligent lead prioritization.
- Salesforce CRM integration.
- Agentforce-based recommendations.
- Workflow automation using Salesforce Flow.
- Real-time customer insights.

---

## 5. Functional Requirements

The system should provide the following functionality:

- User authentication.
- Lead creation and management.
- Customer account management.
- AI-based lead scoring.
- Sales dashboard.
- Automated notifications.
- Lead assignment automation.
- Reporting and analytics.

---

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements

The system should satisfy the following:

- High availability.
- Secure data storage.
- Fast response time.
- Scalability.
- Easy maintenance.
- User-friendly interface.
- Reliable performance.

---

## 7. Stakeholders

The primary stakeholders include:

- Sales Representatives
- Sales Managers
- CRM Administrators
- Business Analysts
- Customers

---

## 8. Expected Benefits

The implementation of this project will:

- Increase sales productivity.
- Improve customer satisfaction.
- Reduce operational costs.
- Improve lead conversion.
- Enhance decision-making.
- Automate repetitive business processes.

---

## Conclusion

The Salesforce AI-Powered Lead Generation System enables organizations to automate lead management, improve customer engagement, and increase sales performance by combining Salesforce CRM with Agentforce AI technologies.

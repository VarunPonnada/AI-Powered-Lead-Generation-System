# Project Scope and Objectives

## Salesforce AI-Powered Lead Generation System

## 1. Project Scope

The Salesforce AI-Powered Lead Generation System focuses on automating lead generation and customer relationship management using Salesforce CRM, Agentforce, and Salesforce Flow.

The project includes customer account management, lead management, AI-powered lead scoring, workflow automation, dashboards, validation rules, and reporting capabilities.

---

## 2. Project Objectives

The main objectives are:

- Automate lead generation.
- Improve lead qualification.
- Increase lead conversion.
- Reduce manual processing.
- Improve customer relationship management.
- Enhance sales team productivity.
- Implement AI-powered recommendations.
- Generate business reports.

---
